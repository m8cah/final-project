Texas Fashion

A vibrant blend of traditional Southern charm, Western influences, and contemporary trends

Key Characteristics of Texas Fashion

Western and Cowboy Influence

Aesthetic: Traditional Western wear, including cowboy hats, boots, and denim.

Materials: Leather, suede, denim, and cotton.

Details: Fringe, embroidery, and studded accents.

Southern Charm

Aesthetic: Feminine, elegant, and often colorful.

Styles: Sundresses, floral prints, and tailored pieces.

Accessories: Pearls, statement jewelry, and wide-brimmed hats.

Bold and Eclectic

Aesthetic: Mixes classic and contemporary styles, often with a vibrant and bold approach.

Colors: Bright hues, classic neutrals, and rich earth tones.

Patterns: Plaid, checks, and bold prints.

Practical and Comfortable

Aesthetic: Casual, functional, and suitable for the Texas climate.

Fit: Relaxed and comfortable yet stylish.
